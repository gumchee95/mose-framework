# Mental Models & Cognitive Tools

Use this skill to apply structured thinking patterns to complex problems.

## Thinking Patterns

### 1. Deconstruction
- **First Principles**: Breaking things down to fundamental truths.
- **Inversion**: Thinking about how to *fail* to find how to *succeed*.

### 2. Strategic Thinking
- **Second-Order Effects**: Evaluating the consequences of consequences.
- **Game Theory**: Analyzing interactions between strategic actors.

### 3. Systems Thinking
- **Feedback Loops**: Identifying reinforcing and balancing loops.
- **Pareto Principle**: Identifying the 20% that drives 80% of results.
